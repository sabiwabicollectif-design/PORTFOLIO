CHAOS CONTACT INSTA
Both Portuguese, Leandro comes from the Numbers and Sara from the Arts. Leandro hates broccoli and to walk barefoot and Sara loves cold pizza and dipping fries in ice cream. They started this investigation almost as a playful experiment — an informal lab, (aka their kitchen) where they test ideas, cross-contaminate knowledge with sillyness. They love to ask questions that often don’t have immediate answers, or sometimes have multiple answers. That's how they decided to take it more seriously, by sharing and discussing it with more people — people who will ask even more questions, lead them down new paths, or simply get lost in it together.
